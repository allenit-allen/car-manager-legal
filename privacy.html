Privacy Policy – Car Manager

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This document explains how information is collected, used, and protected when using the app.

Information collected may include account details, vehicle data entered by the user, images used for VIN scanning, and basic technical data for performance improvement.

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except for essential services required to operate the app such as Google Firebase.

Camera access is used only for VIN scanning purposes and images are not stored without user consent.

By using this app, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
